Step 1: Sign Up

Create an account with BandTogether to start your musical journey. Provide your basic information and verify your email to get started.

Fill out your profile with details about your musical interests, skills, and experience. Upload your profile picture and share your story to attract potential collaborators.

Why Use BandTogether?

Create Your Personalized Profile

Join BandTogether and create a profile that showcases your unique talents. Our platform allows you to highlight your skills, genres, and influences, making it easier for others to find and collaborate with you.

Whether you're looking for gigs, bands, or simply want to jam, BandTogether opens the door to endless opportunities. Explore listings for opportunities near you. Find the perfect fit for your musical journey.

Looking for a stage to perform and meet up? BandTogether makes it easy to find open mic events in your area. Open mics are a great way to gain exposure, practice your live performance skills, and meet other musicians.
