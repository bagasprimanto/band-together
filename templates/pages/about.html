About BandTogether

Photo by Hans Vivek on Unsplash

BandTogether is a website to help hobbyist musicians meet and network with one another. It was created as I myself was having a hard time finding band mates to play with during my busy schedule. The philosophy was a good website equals a better experience networking with fellow musicans. Hopefully you found more joy playing music live as a result of this website!

Feel free to leave a Feedback in our feedback page if you have something to say about our website. We're always looking to improve!

Yours truly,
Demetrius Bagas Primanto
